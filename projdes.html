General Description

The BPSU SIKAT TBI Technology Business Incubation Portal is designed to make it easier for startups in Bataan to get the support they need. It aims to solve the problem of limited access to resources like funding, mentorship, and networking opportunities for new businesses. This platform will help connect entrepreneurs with investors and mentors, and provide a space to manage their applications and progress.

Problem

Startups often struggle to find the right resources, support, and funding to grow their businesses. It can be hard to connect with investors or get proper guidance.

How the System Will Address the Problem

The portal will allow aspiring entrepreneurs (incubatees) to apply for incubation, showcase their business ideas for funding, and get access to mentorship and business support. Investors can easily discover promising startups to fund, while administrators can manage applications, track progress, and communicate with both incubatees and investors.

Proposed Users

  • Incubatees: Entrepreneurs looking for support and funding to grow their businesses.
  • Investors: Individuals or organizations wanting to fund and support startups.
  • Guest: This includes external job seekers and students interested in applying for internships and other jobs at the incubator to gain experience in the startup world.
  • Administrators: BPSU SIKAT TBI staff managing the portal and helping keep everything running smoothly.

General Features

  • Registration & User Management Module: Allows incubatees and accelerators to register for incubation and crowdfunding opportunities, while enabling admins to manage roles, permissions, and accounts for users.
  • Incubatee Mapping & 2D Services Mapping: Helps incubatees locate essential services and navigate the incubation process, while allowing accelerators to find nearby incubatees.
  • Incubatee Explorer & Crowdfunding Module: Allows accelerators to access incubatee profiles for investment and mentorship, while enabling financial support tracking between incubatees and accelerators.
  • Event and Announcement Posting Module: Allows BPSU SIKAT TBI administrators to publish events, announcements, and community activities to keep stakeholders informed and engaged.
  • Inquiry & Messaging System: Allows guests to inquire about opportunities like jobs and internships, while facilitating communication between incubatees, accelerators, and admins.
  • Calendar, Scheduling & SMS Notification Module: Manages events, training, and activities with reminders, while sending automated updates on approvals, events, and announcements.
