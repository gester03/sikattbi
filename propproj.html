About Our Proposed Project

 
 

The BPSU SIKAT TBI (Technology Business Incubator) is a startup support program launched in January 2023 through a partnership between Bataan Peninsula State University (BPSU) and the Department of Science and Technology (DOST). Located at the BPSU Main Campus, it serves as a hub for aspiring entrepreneurs, providing them with mentorship, business development support, funding opportunities, and access to essential facilities to help turn their ideas into successful ventures. The incubator simplifies the registration process for both online and face-to-face applicants, making it easier for startups to join. It also creates a space for collaboration, networking, and innovation, connecting incubatees with investors and industry experts. By integrating technology into its operations, BPSU SIKAT TBI helps startups grow, adapt to market demands, and contribute to the local economy, making it an essential platform for future business leaders in Bataan.
